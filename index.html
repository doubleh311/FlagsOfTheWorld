Introduction

Are you fed up with staying at home during this pandemic?

Do you enjoy traveling around the world?

Do you remember all the flags of the countries you have been to?

Let’s come to our app to have a virtual tour around the world.

This free app will help you recognize and memorize all the flags of countries and territories around the world. The flags will be divided into groups based on geographical features and continents. You will have a chance to study not only the flag but also the official name, the capital and other details of each country.

We have a variety of activities for you to learn such as flashcard, multiple choice, matching, memory matching and writing. Especially, if you complete the activities and gain four out of six stars of each subregion, you will be able to paint your own world map.

Let’s come to our app, sit back, relax and enjoy the tour. Be the master of all the world flags!

Come and enjoy studying with us!
